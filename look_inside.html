The playtesting tool for the rest of us

We are listening to all the developers that are already using the service and we are building the best playtesting platform for the indie dev community

Browser based tool

Your playtesters don't need to install anything, not even register if they don't want to. This allows you to receive recordings from a much wider range of people.

Try it yourself

Automatic upload

The video is automatically uploaded during the recording. You playtester doesn't need to take care of this either.

Try it yourself

Timed comments

The playtester can easily make notes during recording and the notes will be connected to the precise time in the video.

Final feedback and contact details

If the playtesters wants to, they can leave a bottom down comment about the experience. She also can leave a name and email so the developer can engage with her.

Playtester account

Optionally, the playtesters can register in the platform, this way they can create a deepest connection with the developer having the possibility to edit and update the video recordings and feedback they have produced.

All feedback in one place

From the developer perspective you can manage all the video recordings and feedback produced by your playtesters in one place.

See the feedback side by side with the video

In the developer interface you can explore the feedback you have received and, at the same time, analyze the video recording.

Try it in the guest interface

Customize the video recordings

Video recordings can be customized with unique titles and a collection of tags to classify them and to help you to organize your work better.

Video post processing

The generated videos are then processed to optimize size and quality for proper streaming and downloading.

Try it in the guest interface

Download videos

The videos can be downloaded from the platform to have them in your local computer and to do with them what you want.

Sharable feedback

If you want to share one of the video recordings, including all the feedback, with other teammates or even with the whole community you can set it as "public" and a sharable link will be generated. This setting can be undone at any moment and the link will not be valid anymore.

This is guest interface

Free Early Access!

If you are working in an indie project you can ask us for a free early access invitation and start receiving game play recordings from your playtesters. In return we just hope to receive feedback from your experience using Playcocola and if you like it a lot it would be great that you recommend it to other developers to give it visibility :)
