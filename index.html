Playcocola

The online play testing platform specially made for indie developers. It is simple, easy to use and inexpensive.

Easy Use

Just send an invitation link to your play testers. They just have to click on it to start recording video of their session. The video will be sent to you automatically.

Easy Incentivization

Give to your play testers an appreciation reward for their effort. It can be anything from a couple of euros on a steam voucher to just a thank you message.

Easy Management

Visualize the play test sessions directly online or download them to your computer. Recontact your play testers if you need it.

We are Indie

We are ourselves indie devs. We are building this platform to help the specific needs that you and your team have as indie devs.

Pay as you go

No monthly payments. Just pay per play testing video recorded.

Inexpensive

We understand you are not managing big budgets. The price of our service should be affordable to anyone. If you have special budget conditions please let us know.
