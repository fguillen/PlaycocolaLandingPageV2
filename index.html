Playcocola

An online playtesting platform specially made for indie developers. It’s simple, easy to use, and inexpensive.

Easy Use

It’s Easy! Send an invitation link to your playtesters. They just have to click on it to start recording a video of their session. The video recording and comments will be sent to you automatically.

Easy Incentivization

Show your playtesters appreciation by rewarding them for their effort. It can be anything from a thank you message to a couple of euros on a steam voucher.

Easy Management

Watch the playtest sessions directly online or download them to your computer. Contact your playtesters if you need to.

We are Indie

We are indie devs ourselves. We built this platform to help the specific needs that you and your team have as indie devs.

Pay as you go

No commitment. No monthly payments. Just pay per playtesting video recorded.

Inexpensive

We get it! Your budget matters. We feel the price of our service should be affordable to anyone. If you have special budget conditions please let us know.
